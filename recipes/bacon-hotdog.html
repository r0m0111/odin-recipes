Bacon wrapped hot dogs

Decription

If a simple hot dog is too basic for you a hot dog wrappedin juicy and crispy bacon might be just what you were looking for.With only a hot dog sausage and a bun alongside bacon can you make an easy to make and refreshing meal for you and others.

Ingredients

  • Hot dog buns
  • Hot dog sausges
  • Bacon
  • Condoments of choice

Steps

  1. First wrap you bacon across your hot dog starting from the bottom up in a snake like motion
  2. From there heat up your grill or skillet to a high heat and throw hot dogs on
  3. Let each side sit for around 2-4 minutes without moving for a good crust
  4. Add buns to grill or pan and set for 2 minutes each side as well
  5. once done add everything together and top with condoments of choice!
