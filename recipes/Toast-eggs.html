Eggs and Avocado Toast

Decription

This easy to make breakfest takes no time to make while still being a healthy and tasty meal. Its ingredients compose of your standard items of eggs with any seasoning salt and pepper if prefferred and avocado on whole wheat bread. So in case your in a hurry or just want an easy to make breakfest this is the perfect choice for you!

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 20 Grams of avocado
  • Slice of whole wheat bread
  • Salt and pepper
  • Tea spoon of olive oil

Steps

  1. First find a bowl or cup and break both eggs and pour
  2. Stir eggs till completley mixed and add a pinch of salt and pepper
  3. Heat up your pan for approxamitley a minute and pour olive oil along side ggs
  4. Stir eggs for for a minute and set them aside
  5. Toast bread and smere your avocado all over one side
  6. Finally add your scrambled eggs on top and serve with your drink of choice
