Chicken and Rice

Decription

This quick and easy to make meal requires your preferred seasoning alongside white rice, cilantro and of course your chicken.These few ingredients can be found in almost any grocery store near you with little to no cost at all, making it cheap and delicous for all.

Ingredients

  • 177 Grams of Chicken Breast
  • A cup of White Rice
  • A cup of water
  • Preferred seasoning(salt and pepper if none)
  • Tbsp of olive oil
  • Tspn of salt
  • Half a cup of Cilantro

Steps

  1. First clean your white rice in cold water
  2. Toss rice in rice cooker or preferred equipment
  3. Pour your water on the rice and let it cook for approxamitley 20 minutes
  4. Slice chicken into thin slices (around half an inch or less in width)
  5. Gently season your chicken and wait until your pan is hot enough
  6. Pour your olive oil onto the pan and let chicken sit 6 minutes each side
  7. Once your chicken and rice are done cut your cilantro up and toss over rice and mix
  8. Final step is to serve and enjoy!
